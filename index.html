Hannah Morin

Carnegie Mellon University · Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania · hmorin at andrew dot cmu dot edu

I'm concurrently in my third year of pursuing a joint PhD in Engineering and Public Policy, and Materials Science and Engineering. I am advised by Professor Jay Whitacre and Professor Jeremy Michalek. My research investigates the intersection between battery technology and policy. I explore the relationship between electrochemical properties, economics, and human behavior. Presently, my focus is on understanding the impact of fast-charging on electric vehicle battery degradation, employing a blend of experimental and computational methodologies. Looking ahead, I aim to extend my investigations to encompass areas such as vehicle-to-grid technology, grid-scale battery market design, and direct air capture.
